Site Plan: Green Roots Gardening Club

Site Name

Green Roots Gardening Club

This name reflects the site's focus on community gardening, sustainable practices, and connecting local gardeners. It's memorable, descriptive, and fits our goal of targeting gardening enthusiasts and eco-conscious individuals.

Optional domain availability: greenrootsclub.org

Site Purpose

Green Roots Gardening Club provides resources, event information, and gardening tips for members and visitors interested in sustainable gardening. The site will promote eco-friendly gardening practices, offer a platform for members to connect, and showcase local gardening projects and events.

Scenarios

  • What are the upcoming gardening events and workshops?
  • How do I join the Green Roots Gardening Club?
  • What sustainable gardening tips are available for beginners?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Forest Green (#228B22) - Used for headers, buttons, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: Sand Beige (#F5DEB3) - Used for backgrounds and section highlights.

Typography

  • Headings: "Playfair Display", serif
  • Body Text: "Roboto", sans-serif
